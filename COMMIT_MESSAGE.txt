## Role-Based Access Control

Three roles with distinct permissions:
- **Viewer**: Read-only access (view suppliers, dashboard, reporting)
- **Editor**: Full edit access (create/edit/delete suppliers, manage issues/events)
- **Admin**: User management + all editor permissions
